Activities

In Zaandijk there are many activities in which children or the elderly can

participate. These activities range from fun communal swimming to

competitive football matches and much more!

Facilities

Zaandijk has many facilities including supermarkets, pharmacy, dentist,

schools, retirement home and much more. There are also several bus

stops in Zaandijk and a train station.

Environment

Zaandijk has a lot of variety, both in the houses and in the transport

within the village. We are also continuously working on improving the

environment and building new homes.
