Activities for children

There are many activities for children in Zaandijk and the surrounding area. The activities that children can do are indoor swimming, playground(s) where children can play, football field(s) where children can play football and cage football.

These playgrounds are also regularly maintained and, if necessary, the equipment is replaced or new ones are installed. The football fields also contain high-quality artificial grass, which makes playing football on these fields very pleasant.

The indoor swimming park Zaangolf is also a place where children can have fun. This indoor swimming park contains several swimming pools with different depths and grades, and the park also contains several slides. There are many more objects and obstacles for children in the indoor swimming park.

Sport Clubs

There are also several sports clubs in Zaandijk, you can become a member of these sports clubs for a monthly fee. This monthly payment makes you a member of their organization and you can participate during their training sessions and play official matches with your team.

In these sports clubs you also have different classes, of course you always start in the lowest classes and the more experience and the better you become, you will always play a class higher.

The sports clubs you can join are football club (KFC), tennis club (KZTV), korfball club (Koog Zaandijk) and Volleyball association (V.V Zaanstad). Everyone is welcome at these sports clubs and can register. Be Aware! Some sports clubs only allow you to register from a certain age.
