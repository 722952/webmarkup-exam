Schools

Zaandijk has several schools, including a primary school, secondary school and a school for Christian children. All these schools are within walking distance for the residents of Zaandijk. These schools are also highly ranked in the school ranking list, which has a very positive effect on the children their education.

These schools also offer an extensive lesson package so that children who want to go in different directions can take lessons at the same school. Every school also organizes a fun trip with the class once in a while.

That could be going to another country or camping in the woods with the class,it can go either way. Zaandijk is very good in the field of education and is also the ideal place for your child to go to school.

Other Facilities

Zaandijk also has many other facilities such as supermarkets, pharmacy, dentist, etos, gas station and laundry boxes. These facilities are there to provide for all the needs of the residents. As residents of Zaandijk, you do not have to travel far to do your shopping. Since almost all the products you need are available in the village. Almost all medical facilities are also available in the village.
